# AI policy — how AI works on this project

The public, human-readable statement lives at `/ai` (`src/app/ai/page.mdx`).
This file is the operating rule for any AI agent working in this repo.

## The rule

**Naman writes; AI organizes, arranges, and builds.**

- All prose published in Naman's voice — the manifesto, "what we know"
  entries, the home page copy, anything a reader would attribute to him —
  is written by Naman by hand. AI does not draft it, "improve" it, or fill
  it in, even temporarily.
- AI may: build and maintain the code, structure and arrange content Naman
  has written, propose organization (orders, groupings, headings), write
  placeholder scaffolding that is *clearly marked* as placeholder, and write
  developer-facing text (README, docs like this one, commit messages).
- Placeholders must be unmistakable (e.g. `[Placeholder — ...]`) so nothing
  AI-written can be mistaken for Naman's writing or ship unnoticed.
- When Naman supplies text, preserve it verbatim. Quote, don't paraphrase.
  Arranging is allowed; rewording is not, unless he asks for it on a
  specific passage.

## Why

Naman's words: writing yourself is how you take a part of yourself, put it
out, observe it, and understand yourself better. The doing is the point.
AI is leverage for everything around the writing — never a replacement
for it.
