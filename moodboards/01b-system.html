Young Serif has one weight — hierarchy comes from size and space, never faux-bold. Body emphasis uses Instrument Sans 600 or the highlighter. Measures are hard limits: display 16ch, body 62ch.

Spacing — one scale, s1–s6. generous by default; the page breathes like a book
s1 · 8 — inside components
s2 · 16 — between related lines
s3 · 28 — paragraph blocks, component padding
s4 · 48 — between sub-sections
s5 · 80 — between page sections
s6 · 120 — around the hero, chapter breaks

Nothing sits between steps. On mobile every step drops one notch (s5→s4, s6→s5). Content column: max 680px, padding 24px — the site is a single column everywhere; no grids to collapse.

Responsiveness — rules, not breakpoints

One column at every size — nothing rearranges, everything just breathes less. Type scales with clamp(), never with media-query jumps. Spacing drops one step below 640px. Pen marks (circles, underlines) scale with their text because they live in em units. Margin notes float in the right margin on wide screens and tuck under their paragraph on narrow ones. Tap targets never shrink below 44px.

This board is itself the test — resize it. The system has no “mobile version”; it’s the same letter read on smaller paper.
