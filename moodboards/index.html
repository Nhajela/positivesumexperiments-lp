Moodboards

Four directions for positivesumexperiments.com — each built from a different load-bearing idea in the manifesto. Open each in its own tab.

01 — The Declaration The manifesto as a printed statement. The + glyph is the entire identity system. Paper, ink, one electric cobalt. Quietest and most timeless. 02 — The Ledger Business as an economic model, taken literally: ruled paper, banker's green and red ink, the 30-day rule as an actual ledger row, "default alive" as a running balance. 03 — The Game Axiom two as a game-theory seminar: chalk on blackboard green, a 2×2 payoff matrix with three cells crossed out. Riskiest — commits fully to one metaphor. 04 — Shipped Clarity comes from action: visible edits, rubber stamps carrying real state, safety yellow highlighter. Loudest; the working-document honesty is the aesthetic.
