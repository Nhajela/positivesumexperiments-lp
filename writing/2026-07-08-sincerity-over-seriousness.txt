6. Sincerity over Seriousness

Last but not the least, so much so that this should really be a disclaimer at the start of this page.

We are all going to die.
It won't matter.

Not marble, nor guided momuments, nor these rhymes shall outlive anything. 

Don't do it because it matters in some grand scheme of things. It doesn't. 
Do it, play the game, purely for the love of playing. 
For the love of every curveball the game throws at you.

Afterall, it is a game. 

Worry less, smile more, embrace the game more. 
The more you embrace the game the more chances you'll have to appreciate it, in all aspects.
This is Sincerity. 

What is not demanded of you, is Seriousness. 
Seriousness, worry, fear, and many more things are merely forgetting the fact that this is a game afterall.

Have fun, you be dead before you know it.
